FAQs

01What is an AI video?

An AI video is content built around a fully AI-generated persona instead of a live actor. We build it to look and feel like real UGC, whether it's headed to organic posts, paid ads, or product pages.

02What is the difference between a video request and an animation request?

A video request includes full concept development: the idea, story, and direction for something like an ad, a content series, or a storyboard-driven piece. An animation request is execution only, bringing a concept you already have to life through animation or editing. Not sure which one fits? Describe your project in the brief and we'll scope it from there.

03What is an AI image?

An AI image is a photorealistic image built for your brand, product, or campaign, created without a physical photo shoot.

04What is a digital twin?

A digital twin is a custom AI version of you, or of your brand's talent, built from your likeness. It can show up in content and marketing, or act as an ongoing virtual presence for your brand.
